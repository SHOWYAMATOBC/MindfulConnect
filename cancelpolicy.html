Cancellation Policy

We understand that circumstances can change. If you need to cancel your order, we are here to assist you.

Cancellation Terms

You may cancel your order within 24 hours of purchase for a full refund. After 24 hours, cancellations may incur a fee depending on the product.

How to Cancel

1. Email us at support@yourwebsite.com to request a cancellation.

2. Include your order number and reason for cancellation in your email.

3. If your order has not yet shipped, we will process your cancellation and issue a full refund.

Refund Process

If your cancellation is successful, refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once the refund has been issued.
